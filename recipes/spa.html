Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Description

Rich, thick, incredible flavour in the sauce even if you only have time for a quick 20 minute simmer. Though if you can slow cook for a couple of hours, it really takes it to another level!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped (brown, yellow, or white)
  • 500 g beef mince (ground beef)
  • 1/2 cup (125 ml) dry red wine
  • 2 beef bouillon cubes, crumbled or granulated
  • 800 g can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper

To Serve

  • 400 g spaghetti
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium high heat
  2. Add onion and garlic, cook for 3 minutes until light golden and softened
  3. Turn heat up high and add beef
  4. Add red wine. Cook for 1 minute until alcohol smell is gone
  5. Add remaining ingredients except salt and pepper
  6. Cook for 20-30 minutes, add water if sauce gets too thick
  7. Adjust salt and pepper
