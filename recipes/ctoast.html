Cinnamon Toast Recipe

Description

Yummy cinnamon toast makes a great breakfast or snack!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 teaspoons butter or margarine
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Toast bread slices
  2. Spread butter onto one side
  3. Stir together sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl
  4. Sprinkle over hot, buttered toast
